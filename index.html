MOON PHASE BLACKJACK

Where Lunar Forces Determine Your Destiny

The Game

Moon Phase Blackjack is a celestial twist on classic Blackjack. Get closer to 21 than the dealer without going over. Each hand shifts the moon phase, bringing a new rule or reward.

Basic Rules

  • Card values: 2-10 face value, J/Q/K = 10, A = 1 or 11
  • Goal: beat the dealer total without going over 21
  • Blackjack: 21 with first two cards pays 1.5x
  • Hit: draw another card
  • Stand: keep your hand and let the dealer play
  • Double down: double your bet for one more card (first turn only)
  • Bust: go over 21 and lose the hand

Payouts

  • Blackjack: 1.5x your bet (3x on Last Quarter)
  • Regular win: 1x your bet (2x on Last Quarter)
  • Push (tie): return your bet
  • Loss: lose your bet
